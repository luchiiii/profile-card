About Me

My Journey

Hi, I'm Okwuosa Oluchi, a passionate front-end developer dedicated to creating accessible and user-friendly web experiences. My journey into tech started with curiosity about how beautiful websites are built, and it has grown into a deep passion for crafting digital experiences that everyone can enjoy.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me exploring skincare and fashion content, diving into African literature novels, or learning about personal finance. I believe in continuous learning and bringing creativity into everything I do.

My Goals in This Program

Joining this program is a significant step in my development journey. Here's what I aim to achieve:

  • Master modern frontend frameworks and best practices
  • Build real-world projects that solve actual problems
  • Collaborate with talented developers and learn from their experiences
  • Strengthen my understanding of accessibility and inclusive design
  • Develop confidence in my technical abilities and decision-making
  • Create a strong portfolio that showcases my skills and creativity

Areas I'm Working to Improve

Growth comes from acknowledging areas where we need improvement. Here are the areas where I'm building more confidence:

  • Complex JavaScript algorithms: While I'm comfortable with frontend logic, I'm working on strengthening my problem-solving skills with more complex algorithms and data structures.
  • Backend integration: I want to become more confident in connecting frontend applications with APIs and understanding the full stack.
  • Speaking up in technical discussions: Sometimes I second-guess myself in group settings. I'm working on trusting my knowledge and contributing more actively.
  • Performance optimization: I want to deepen my understanding of web performance metrics and optimization techniques.
